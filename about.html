About

I am a University of Leeds graduate, currently finishing of my masters at Goldsmiths, London. I love exploring the different ways people interact with technology. I create digital and physical projects, such as: audio-visual installations, musical instruments, digital prints, and generative art. Most work is done in openFrameworks (C++) or MaxMSP, but I also have experience in Java, Javascript and HTML/CSS.

I am also an avid musician and always try to have a musical project on the go. One of my current projects is using a live coding envrionment called Orca, which I use to create generative and glitchy electronic compositions.
